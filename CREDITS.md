## CoreAlign visual system

- Scientific editorial layout; maximum reading width and strong section hierarchy
- Neutral paper/ink surfaces with teal for action and amber for review/attention
- Geist Sans + Geist Mono
- Motion limited to meaningful orientation cues and disabled with reduced-motion preferences
- WCAG-oriented contrast, keyboard-focusable controls, semantic landmarks, and bilingual copy
